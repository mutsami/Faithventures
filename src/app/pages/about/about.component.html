Explore
our history

From humble beginnings back in 2001, FVCA's 12 pioneer students started learning inside a corrugated iron sheet classroom that had been restored from what once used to be a store. With time, FVCA has continued to grow, receiving student enrollment from diverse nationalities backgrounds.

The school's infrastructure has considerable expanded to cater for the growing demand of students. From its inception, the school has played a key role as a model for other schools that have started in the country using the ACE curriculum. We hope to not only continue doing this, but also to be the trend setter in the wider East African region.
