Download application form

After receiving an enquiry for admission the following procedure is followed:

  • Download The Application form or get from the school.
  • Submit your application through our email, admissions@faithventures.sc.ke or at our school reception.
  • The school office will email for an interview (both parents and pupil) with the School Principal.
  • Parents will be notified of acceptance via email.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to email (admissions@faithventures.sc.ke) or call (0735-190-198). You are also welcome to make an appointment with the Admissions Office to discuss any questions you may have or to take a tour of the school. We’d be delighted to speak with you further and help you with your application process.
